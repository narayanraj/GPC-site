Our Vision @ GPC
To be recognized by Industrial & Social Fraternity as the Technical Institute which imparts Quality Technical Education & provides trained manpower as per the set standards by developing the students with right attitude & aptitude confident enough to stand on their own feet.

Our Mission @ GPC
To impart Quality Technical Education to student fraternity of Goa with more emphasis on educating of rural & economically backward students so that these students are trained with required competencies to be readily absorbed by industry and are developed to face the challenges of life with right attitude & respect for the nation, thereby helping in development of the nation.
